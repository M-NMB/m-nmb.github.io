<div class="research-page">
  <p class="research-lead">
    My research focuses on complex anion materials, epitaxial thin films, and the electronic and magnetic phenomena that emerge when anion chemistry, lattice structure, and transition-metal states are tuned together.
  </p>

  <section class="research-section">
    <h2>Complex Anion Chemistry</h2>
    <p>
      I am interested in oxyhydrides, oxyfluorides, and oxynitrides, where mixed-anion coordination can create electronic states and local structures that are difficult to access in conventional oxides.
    </p>
    <ul class="research-keywords">
      <li>oxyhydrides</li>
      <li>oxyfluorides</li>
      <li>oxynitrides</li>
      <li>topotactic reactions</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="research-section">
    <h2>Thin Films and Interfaces</h2>
    <p>
      Epitaxial thin films provide a platform for controlling strain, dimensionality, and reaction pathways, making it possible to stabilize metastable phases and probe structure-property relationships.
    </p>
    <ul class="research-keywords">
      <li>epitaxial growth</li>
      <li>metastable phases</li>
      <li>strain control</li>
      <li>interfaces</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="research-section">
    <h2>Electronic and Magnetic Properties</h2>
    <p>
      I study how intersite charge transfer, spin-orbit coupling, and local coordination influence magnetic anisotropy and functional responses in transition-metal compounds.
    </p>
    <ul class="research-keywords">
      <li>charge transfer</li>
      <li>spin-orbit coupling</li>
      <li>magnetic anisotropy</li>
      <li>transition-metal oxides</li>
    </ul>
  </section>

  <section class="research-section">
    <h2>Computational Materials Science</h2>
    <p>
      First-principles calculations are used alongside experiments to interpret electronic structure, compare candidate mechanisms, and guide the design of new materials.
    </p>
    <ul class="research-keywords">
      <li>DFT</li>
      <li>electronic structure</li>
      <li>materials design</li>
      <li>experiment-theory links</li>
    </ul>
  </section>
</div>
